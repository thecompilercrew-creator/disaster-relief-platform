# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

This document answers common questions about the Disaster Relief Platform for users, contributors, and evaluators.

---

## General

### What is this platform?
The Disaster Relief Platform is a web-based system designed to coordinate help during disasters by connecting people in need, volunteers, and resource providers through real-time maps, structured requests, and AI assistance.

---

### Who can use this platform?
- Disaster-affected individuals
- Volunteers
- NGOs and relief organizations
- Community groups
- Hackathon and civic-tech deployments

Anyone with internet access can use the platform.

---

### Is this an official emergency service?
No. This platform **does not replace government or emergency services**. In life-threatening situations, users should always contact local emergency authorities.

---

## Usage

### Do I need technical knowledge to use the app?
No. The application is designed for **non-technical users**. All actions are performed using buttons, forms, and maps.

---

### How do I request help?
1. Log in
2. Go to the **Requests** tab
3. Click **Add Request**
4. Fill in the form and submit

Your request will appear on the map for volunteers to see.

---

### How do I volunteer?
Browse requests under the **Requests** tab and click **Volunteer** on a request you want to help with. Contact details become visible only after volunteering.

---

### Can I edit a request or resource?
Yes. You can edit your own requests and resources, including fixing vague addresses directly from the map popup.

---

## Map & Location

### Why is my address marked as vague or invalid?
Some addresses cannot be accurately converted into map coordinates. The platform flags these so users can edit and improve location accuracy.

---

### Why can’t I see markers on the map?
Possible reasons:
- Poor internet connection
- Map tiles blocked by network
- Requests/resources missing valid coordinates

Refreshing the page usually resolves this.

---

## News & AI

### Why does the News tab sometimes fail to load?
The News tab relies on external RSS feeds. If the provider rate-limits or blocks requests, news may temporarily fail to load.

---

### What is the Gemini AI assistant used for?
Gemini provides guidance, planning help, and answers disaster-related questions. It does **not** provide medical or legal advice.

---

## Security & Privacy

### Who can see my contact details?
Contact information is only visible to users who volunteer for a request, helping protect privacy.

---

### Is my data safe?
The platform uses authentication, encrypted passwords, and controlled access. However, users should avoid sharing highly sensitive information.

---

## Technical

### Is the project open source?
Yes. The project is open-source and welcomes contributions.

---

### What technologies are used?
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: MongoDB
- Maps: Leaflet, OpenStreetMap
- AI: Google Gemini

## Limitations

### What are the current limitations?
- No offline mode
- No SMS alerts yet
- Depends on third-party APIs for maps and news

These are planned for future development.
